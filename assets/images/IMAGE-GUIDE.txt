Clean Sweep Image Guide
=======================

The official Clean Sweep logo is stored at:

assets/logo/full-logo-transparent.png

Use the transparent full logo in footers and the transparent symbol/wordmark crops in compact headers and mobile navigation.
Keep logo files optimized for the web and avoid spaces in file names.

The layout is designed to preserve the logo's proportions without stretching.

Recommended photograph files
----------------------------

1. street-sweeper-hero.webp
   Recommended size: 1920 x 1200
   Appears in: Hero sections across the concepts
   Use: A wide professional photo of a street sweeper at a clean commercial property.
   Notes: Avoid text, fake company logos, readable license plates, watermarks, or identifiable people.

2. commercial-sweeping.webp
   Recommended size: 1400 x 900
   Appears in: Services and service-area sections
   Use: A clean parking lot, commercial property, or active sweeping service image.
   Notes: Landscape orientation works best.

3. street-sweeping-detail.webp
   Recommended size: 1400 x 1000
   Appears in: Why Choose Clean Sweep sections
   Use: A close detail of sweeping equipment, curb-line service, or a maintained exterior area.
   Notes: Keep the image sharp and industrial, not overly staged.

How to replace images
---------------------

1. Export each replacement image using the file name above.
2. Place the file in this folder: assets/images/
3. Keep images optimized for the web. WebP is recommended for photos.
4. Refresh the browser and confirm the image is not stretched or cropped poorly.

The current photos are temporary custom-generated placeholders and should be replaced with real company photography when the business owner has final assets.
